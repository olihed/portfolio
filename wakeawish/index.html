Wake A Wish

Wake a Wish is a digital alarm clock, that at the push of the snooze button donates money to an organization of the users choice. This concept was produced during the three weeks we worked under our agency This Was Broken. The application allows for non-profits to take part in a completely new way of doing business, while tapping into a new revenue stream.

We wanted to challenge the current donation model and the way people perceive non-profits. Our insight was that everyday people snooze, a lot. That behavior turned into the foundation for the overall strategy of the concept. And ultimately we wanted people to feel good and do good at the same time.
